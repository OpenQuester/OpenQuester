# Privacy Policy for OpenQuester

**Last Updated: January 2, 2026**

## Introduction

OpenQuester ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our multiplayer quiz game application ("Application" or "Service").

This policy applies to all users of OpenQuester, available on mobile platforms (Google Play Store, Apple App Store) and other supported platforms.

By using OpenQuester, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information when you use our Service:

#### Account Information
- **Username**: A unique identifier chosen by you or auto-generated for guest accounts
- **Display Name**: Your preferred name to be shown to other users
- **Email Address**: Collected only when you authenticate via Discord OAuth (optional)
- **Discord ID**: Your Discord user identifier when using Discord authentication
- **Birthday**: Optional field for age verification or personalization
- **Avatar/Profile Picture**: Profile image either uploaded by you or retrieved from Discord

#### Authentication Types
- **Discord OAuth**: When you log in with Discord, we receive your Discord profile information including username, email (if provided), avatar, and Discord ID
- **Guest Access**: Temporary accounts with auto-generated usernames and no email requirement

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

#### Usage Data
- Game session data (games played, scores, participation statistics)
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- Session information (login times, session duration)
- Network information (IP address, connection type)

#### Technical Data
- Application version
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics

### 3. Game-Related Data

- Quiz questions answered
- Game room participation
- Player interactions (chat messages within game rooms)
- User-created content packages (themes, questions, media)
- Game statistics and leaderboard information

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Service Provision
- Create and manage user accounts
- Authenticate users and maintain sessions
- Enable multiplayer game functionality
- Display user profiles and statistics
- Synchronize game state across players

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve game features
- Debug technical issues and fix bugs
- Optimize performance and user experience
- Develop new features based on user behavior

### Communication
- Send important service announcements
- Respond to user inquiries and support requests
- Notify users of updates or changes to our Service

### Security and Compliance
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect user rights and safety

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- User data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption
- Profile images and media are stored using S3-compatible object storage (MinIO)
- Session data is managed through Redis for optimal performance
- User information is stored in PostgreSQL databases with appropriate access controls

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including:
- Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Secure authentication mechanisms (OAuth 2.0, session management)
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication for administrative functions
- Secure backup procedures

### Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained while account remains active
- Guest accounts: May be deleted after periods of inactivity
- Deleted accounts: Personal information deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
- Game statistics: May be retained in anonymized form for analytics

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing
We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

#### With Other Users
- Your username, display name, and avatar are visible to other players in game rooms
- Your game statistics may be displayed on leaderboards
- User-created content packages are publicly visible

#### Service Providers
- Cloud hosting providers (for infrastructure)
- Analytics services (for app performance monitoring)
- Authentication providers (Discord for OAuth)

#### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations or court orders
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent fraud or illegal activities
- Respond to government requests

## Third-Party Services

### Discord OAuth
When you choose to authenticate via Discord, we use Discord's OAuth 2.0 service. Please review [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) to understand how they handle your data.

### External Links
Our Application may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

## Children's Privacy

OpenQuester is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.
